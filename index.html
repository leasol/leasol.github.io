Privacy Policy

Last Updated: 07.07.2025

Lea Sol Bokobza (“Lea”, “I”, “me”, or “my”) develops and publishes mobile game titles (“Games”) for devices through Google Play and the Apple App Store.

This Privacy Policy explains how I collect, use, and share your information when you use my Games. I take your privacy seriously and aim to be transparent about my data practices.

About Me

I am an independent game developer. This Privacy Policy applies to all mobile Games developed and published by Lea Sol Bokobza, whether available on Android or iOS platforms.

Scope

This Privacy Policy applies to all personal data collected and processed through my Games. It does not apply to other websites, services, or third-party content that may be linked within the Games.

Because my Games are available globally, this policy is designed to comply with applicable data protection laws, including:

  • The General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR) for users in the EU/EEA, UK, and Switzerland
  • The California Consumer Privacy Act (CCPA) for residents of California, USA

Information I Collect

1. Information Collected Automatically Through Games

This includes information collected directly through the app or via analytics and advertising SDKs:

  • Device identifiers (e.g., Advertising ID / IDFA / IDFV)
  • IP address
  • Device type, OS, and version
  • App version and usage data
  • Crash reports and diagnostic data
  • In-game interactions and session length
  • Transaction and purchase data (if applicable)
  • Language and time zone
  • Mobile carrier and network type

2. Information from Advertising & Analytics Partners

  • Ad interactions and view data
  • Attributed ad revenue
  • User behavior profiles (for personalized ads if consented)

How I Use the Information

I may use your information for the following purposes:

  • Operate and improve the Games
  • Analyze usage and performance
  • Fix bugs and enhance functionality
  • Serve in-game ads (personalized if allowed)
  • Provide support and respond to inquiries
  • Prevent fraud and ensure app security
  • Comply with legal obligations

Maximum Information Retention Period

Information is retained only as long as necessary for the purposes listed above, unless a longer period is legally required or permitted.

How I Share Your Information

I may share data with the following:

  • Ad networks & marketing partners (e.g., AdMob, Unity Ads)
  • Analytics providers (e.g., Firebase, GameAnalytics)
  • Third-party service providers (e.g., crash reporting, storage)
  • Legal authorities, if required to comply with laws

How to Control Your Information

Opt-out of Personalized Ads

You can limit personalized ads via your device settings:

  • iOS: Settings > Privacy > Tracking > Turn off “Allow Apps to Request to Track”
  • Android: Settings > Google > Ads > Opt out of Ads Personalization

GDPR Rights

If you're located in the EEA, UK, or Switzerland, you have the right to:

  • Access your personal data
  • Request correction or deletion
  • Restrict or object to processing
  • Data portability (in certain cases)
  • Withdraw consent where applicable

To exercise these rights, contact me at: soldarcompany@gmail.com

CCPA Rights

California residents have the right to:

  • Know what data is collected and how it's used
  • Request deletion of their data
  • Opt out of the sale of personal data (Note: I do not sell data)
  • Not be discriminated against for exercising privacy rights

To make a request, email: soldarcompany@gmail.com

Children’s Privacy

I do not knowingly collect data from children under 13 (or 16 in some jurisdictions). If you believe your child provided personal information, contact me and I will delete it.

International Transfers

Your data may be processed in countries outside of your own. I follow applicable laws and implement safeguards for international data transfers.

Changes to This Policy

I may update this Privacy Policy occasionally. Significant changes will be communicated within the app or store listing. The “Last Updated” date reflects the latest revision.
